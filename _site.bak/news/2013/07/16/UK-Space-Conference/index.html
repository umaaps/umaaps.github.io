UK Space Conference

Jul 16, 2013
news
StrathSEDS

The 2013 UK Space Conference, focused on realising the UK's ambitions in space - including making a global impact with science and research, contributing to the UK's economic growth by developing new commercial applications and businesses and developing the interactions between these activities.

A scout team discussing the inflated shape of their space deployable.

Scouts from various UK scout organisations trying to build their own space inflatable. An exercise involving team work, design, fabrication and testing.

Photo credit: ESA/Thomas Sinn

General public visiting the students' education stand

The second day the UK Space Conference was open to families. Over 400 interested visitors stopped by to get informed about our REXUS/BEXUS experiments and other ESA Education Office projects like Spin Your Thesis, Drop Your Thesis and ESEO. REXUS13's experiment StrathSat-R can be seen in the background of the picture.

General Information

The University of Manitoba American Association of Pharmaceutical Scientists (AAPS) Student Chapter is the official local AAPS student chapter Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada.

It has continued to focus on the expasion of membership to all departments located at the University of Manitoba Health Science Campus. Many students from diverse biomedical departments have found the benefits of AAPS student chapter membership through a novel approach to monthly events we recently implemented. Studetn chapter resources are frequently used to sponsor lunches with influential scientit presenters from the various fields or departments represented by our student membership; giving a unique opportunity to network with and gain valuable career development input from leaders in the pharmaceutical sciences.


Our Goals and Objectives

  • Expand our memberships beyond the faculties of pharmacy, medicine, and physiology
  • Continue to bring innovative speakers from academia and industry and sponsor lunches to facilitate networking apportunities and professional development for the students
  • Continue to hold AAPS membership gatherings
  • Be more involved in our local community
  • Connecting with neighboring AAPS chapters

UM AAPS Student Chapter is funded and supported by AAPS and College of Pharmacy, Faculty of Health Sciences, University of Manitoba.
